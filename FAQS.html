FAQS

1 ) What is Aamnaz Tailor ?

Aamnaz Tailor provides doorstep tailoring services to men without having to step out of the house. Whether it is Shirts, Kurta, Wedding suits, Pants and Punjabi we help you to get your dress material stitched as per your measurement garment by professional tailors. You can schedule a free pickup service when you place an order. Our pickup team will visit your house to collect the dress material and the measurement garment. It will take 10 working days to stitch,finish and deliver your tailored outfit.

2 ) Why Aamnaz Tailor and not other tailoring shop ?

Does bad fit and untimely deliveries from your local tailor frustrate you ? Do you find it inconvenient to go, deliver and pickup your orders? Will now you have a choice. Just place your order online at your convenience and schedule a free pickup & leave the rest with us. It you have difficulty placing your order, Just call us. We will help you place your order.

3 ) What are the garment types that you offer stitching service ?

Currently, we offer eight types of garment Wedding Suits, Waistcoat, Nehru Jacket, Shirt, Kurta, Pathani Kurta, Pant, and Pajama.

4 ) Do you send a tailor to my house to take my measurement?

No. You will have to provide a perfectly fitting garment along with the dress material so that we can stitch it as per your measurements.

5 ) How much dose this service cost ?

The cost various based on your stitching requirement. Please refer to the pricing section above for more details.

6 ) Are there any pickup and delivery charges ?

No. We offer free pickup & delivery. Currently our service is available in Asansol city and we will be coming to other cities soon.

7 ) Do you alter in case the stitched dress does not fit me well ?

It is very unlikely to happen as we take the measurement garment from you before we start stitching the dress material. However, it is happens, we will alter it as per your need free of cost.

8 ) Awesome. Who is behind this service ?

We are a team of professional, including a fashion design expert. We are passionate about bringing change to the unprofessional world of tailoring service in India. We belive that our process, technology, and professionally managed stitching centre is going to bring a smile on your face, forever.
